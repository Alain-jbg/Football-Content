About Ligi Open

At Ligi Open, we believe in revolutionizing the way football is funded and supported. Our Open Financing Initiative represents a groundbreaking approach to sustaining and developing the sport we all love, football. With open financing, we're not just asking for financial support; we're inviting you to be an integral part of our journey. This initiative is about more than just money; it's about transparency, community involvement, and the collective passion that drives us forward. Join us in building a brighter future for football in kenya.
